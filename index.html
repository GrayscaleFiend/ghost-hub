What's an Ukagaka/Ghost?

An Ukagaka, or ghost, is a computer mascot that can talk to you, manage a calendar, take out your recycling bin, and much more! Some can also interact with each other. They run on the baseware SSP (Sakurascript Player) and originated as a Japanese program which can be downloaded on the left sidebar of this website here: ssp.shillest.net.
